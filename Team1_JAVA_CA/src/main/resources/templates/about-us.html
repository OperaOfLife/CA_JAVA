About Us

NSIU aspires to be the inspiration for invention and enterprise amongst our students. Here at NSIU, we believe in independence and innovation. Our sheer focus is on the spirit of quality education to shape the leaders of the future and create a positive impact on society. As the Chinese proverb goes: “If you give a man a fish, you feed him for a day – if you teach him how to fish, you feed him for a lifetime.” NSIU aims to equip students with relevant skills to enable them to thrive and excel as leaders of honour.

NSIU is home to multiple world class institutes and research centres – the National Fishing and Boating Institute, Aquatic Life Sciences Engineering, Ocean Studies Institute and the Environment and Energy Research Centre.

Consistently ranked amongst the world’s top 50 universities for the past 5 years, NSIU is notably one of the world’s leading universities. We uphold our teachings with honour and believe in synergy between students and educators for a fulfilling education journey with us.
